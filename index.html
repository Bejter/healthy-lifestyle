Nutrition

Eating a balanced diet is key to maintaining overall health. Include plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins in your meals.

  • Eat a variety of colorful vegetables
  • Stay hydrated by drinking water regularly
  • Limit processed foods and sugar intake
Learn More About Nutrition

Exercise

Physical activity is essential for a healthy body and mind. Incorporate both strength training and cardiovascular exercises into your routine.

  • Strength training 2-3 times a week
  • Cardio exercise (running, cycling, swimming) for at least 30 minutes a day
  • Stretch and improve flexibility regularly
Learn More About Exercise

Sleep

Getting enough quality sleep is crucial for recovery, mood, and cognitive function. Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep each night.

  • Maintain a consistent sleep schedule
  • Ensure a comfortable sleeping environment
  • Avoid caffeine and screens before bed
